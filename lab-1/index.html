About Daffodil International University

Daffodil International University (DIU) is one of the leading private universities in Bangladesh, established in 2002 under the Private University Act. The university is dedicated to providing quality education, innovation, and research to prepare students for global challenges.

DIU offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in fields such as Engineering, Business, Science, Arts, and Information Technology.

  • Practical and project-based learning
  • Digital campus and smart classrooms
  • Research and innovation culture
  • International collaborations and exchange programs

Its main campus is located at Daffodil Smart City, Ashulia, Dhaka, which provides a modern and eco-friendly environment for learning and innovation.

DIU Students Win Robotics Competition

Students from Daffodil International University (DIU) have clinched first place in the prestigious National Robotics Challenge 2025, held at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET). The competition featured over 50 universities from across the country, all showcasing cutting-edge robotics technologies. The winning DIU team designed an autonomous robot capable of performing complex tasks such as object detection, precise navigation, and obstacle avoidance. The robot integrated AI-based decision-making algorithms and IoT-enabled sensors, allowing it to adapt to changing environments in real time. According to the team leader, Md. Rahim, “We focused on creating a robot that could work efficiently in unpredictable situations. This victory is a testament to the hard work and innovative spirit of our team.” The judges praised DIU’s entry for its technical sophistication, creativity, and practical applicability. Beyond the trophy, the team also received scholarships and internship offers from top tech companies in Bangladesh. DIU Vice-Chancellor, Professor Dr. Md. Kamal Hossain, congratulated the students and emphasized the university’s commitment to nurturing innovation and research. He added, “Our students are not only excelling academically but also making a mark on the national and international stage.” The team hopes to represent Bangladesh in upcoming international robotics competitions, aiming to showcase their talent on a global platform.

New Research Center Opened

Daffodil International University (DIU) has officially inaugurated a state-of-the-art Research and Innovation Center aimed at fostering creativity, hands-on learning, and advanced research among students. The center is equipped with modern laboratories, 3D printing facilities, robotics kits, AI development labs, and collaborative workspaces, providing students with a professional environment to develop innovative projects. At the inauguration ceremony, DIU Vice-Chancellor, Professor Dr. Md. Kamal Hossain, highlighted the importance of practical experience in education: “Our goal is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application. This center will empower students to bring their ideas to life and contribute to technological advancements in Bangladesh.” The center will support research in areas such as Artificial Intelligence, IoT, Robotics, Renewable Energy, and Software Development. Students will also have access to mentorship programs, workshops, and international collaborations to help turn their projects into viable products. According to Ms. Ayesha Rahman, a computer engineering student, “This facility is a game-changer for students. It allows us to experiment, learn, and innovate in ways that were not possible before.” The new Research and Innovation Center reinforces DIU’s commitment to building a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship, preparing students for careers in the global technology sector.

Library Digitization Project

Daffodil International University (DIU) has launched a comprehensive online digital library aimed at enhancing research and learning opportunities for students and faculty. The digital library provides access to thousands of e-books, academic journals, research papers, and reference materials across multiple disciplines, making high-quality resources available anytime, anywhere. The platform includes advanced search functionality, personalized recommendations, and topic-based collections, allowing users to efficiently locate the information they need. Students can now download materials for offline reading, annotate resources, and collaborate with peers on research projects. According to DIU’s Librarian-in-Charge, Ms. Nusrat Jahan, “This digitization initiative ensures that students and researchers have seamless access to knowledge without being limited by physical library hours or availability. It is part of our effort to modernize learning and support academic excellence.” The digital library also connects to international academic databases, giving students exposure to global research trends and publications. Additionally, workshops and training sessions are being conducted to help students maximize the benefits of the digital resources. With the Library Digitization Project, DIU aims to empower students, promote self-directed learning, and foster a culture of continuous academic growth.

Smart Campus Initiative

Daffodil International University (DIU) has launched a Smart Campus Initiative aimed at transforming the campus into a technologically advanced and energy-efficient environment. The initiative leverages IoT-based systems, automation technologies, and smart monitoring tools to enhance the overall campus experience for students, faculty, and staff. The implemented systems include automated lighting, climate control, security monitoring, smart attendance tracking, and real-time energy management. These measures are designed to optimize energy usage, reduce operational costs, and provide a sustainable campus ecosystem. According to DIU’s Director of Facilities, Mr. Asif Rahman, “The Smart Campus Initiative is part of our vision to integrate technology with campus life. Students can benefit from a safer, greener, and more efficient environment while gaining exposure to advanced IoT applications.” In addition to operational efficiency, the initiative also serves as a learning platform for students studying IoT, AI, and automation. Workshops and practical labs allow students to experiment with real-world smart systems, fostering innovation and hands-on experience. The project aligns with DIU’s goal of creating a forward-thinking, eco-friendly campus while promoting research and development in emerging technologies.

International Collaboration with Japan

Daffodil International University (DIU) has signed a strategic partnership with a leading Japanese university to foster international collaboration in education and research. The agreement focuses on student exchange programs, faculty collaboration, and joint research initiatives, particularly in the fields of Artificial Intelligence (AI), robotics, and data science. Under the partnership, DIU students will have the opportunity to study abroad in Japan for a semester, participate in joint workshops, and work on cutting-edge research projects alongside Japanese students and faculty. Similarly, Japanese students and researchers will visit DIU to contribute to collaborative projects, creating a mutually beneficial learning environment. According to Professor Dr. Md. Kamal Hossain, Vice-Chancellor of DIU, “This collaboration opens doors for our students to gain international exposure, enhance their research skills, and build networks that will be valuable for their professional growth.” The agreement also includes plans for joint publications, AI innovation labs, and technology seminars, aimed at advancing research capabilities and promoting cross-cultural academic exchange. Students are expected to gain hands-on experience with advanced AI systems and participate in international competitions and conferences. This collaboration reinforces DIU’s commitment to global partnerships, innovation-driven learning, and excellence in higher education, preparing students to compete on a worldwide stage.
